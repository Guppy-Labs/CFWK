About Cute Fish With Knives

Cute Fish With Knives (CFWK) is a free-to-play multiplayer browser game where players find fish and discover treasures. Featuring retro pixel art graphics and real-time multiplayer combat, CFWK combines friendship with intense battles. Lead your story, battle friends, and join a growing community of players and creators.

Key Features

  • Free-to-play browser game - no download required
  • Custom map creator - design and share your own maps
  • Real-time multiplayer combat
  • Retro pixel art style
  • Isometric gameplay perspective
  • Growing community of players and creators
